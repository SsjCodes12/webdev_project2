Balloon Festival

Here in New Mexico we have balloon festivals, an event where balloons are sent into the sky with all kinds of unique styles and colors. These events are complete with vendors, food, and other fun activities. When trying to capture some of the moments some I had to employ the use of these photography tips

  • Pick the right time of day
  • Placement of your subject

Nature Photography

For the collection of these photos I went out into my local area. For the icy images I went out in the local park and trail during the winter trying to find subjects to capture. I went out during a cloudy day for consistent light and less shadows. For the flower, and tree trunk image I went to a different trail during the spring. I purposfully chose to find some dramatic shading.

I enjoy photography as a fun hobby of mine when I have the free time. Goin outdoors to find a subject. Maybe it's a landscape one day, some wildlife photos the next, or perhaps more mundane but made interesting with how it's framed. All in all I quite enjoy snapping photos when I can. I look forward to learning more fields outside of Graphic Design like Photography, website building and more. I would like some experience in as many fields as I can learn.
